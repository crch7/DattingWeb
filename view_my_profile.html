Introduction

At Meet&Eat, we are committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy outlines the types of personal data we collect, how we use it, and the steps we take to protect your information.

1. Data Collection

We collect personal data from you when you visit our website, make a reservation, or interact with our services. The types of information we may collect include:

  • Your name, email address, and phone number
  • Information about your device and usage patterns on our website
  • Payment information, if applicable

2. How We Use Your Data

The personal data we collect is used to:

  • Provide and improve our services
  • Process reservations and orders
  • Communicate with you regarding your account or service updates
  • Send promotional content and updates (with your consent)

3. Data Retention

We retain your personal data for as long as necessary to fulfill the purposes outlined in this Privacy Policy. You may request the deletion of your data at any time by contacting us directly.

4. Your Data Protection Rights

You have certain rights regarding your personal data, including the right to:

  • Access your personal data
  • Correct inaccurate data
  • Request the deletion of your data
  • Opt-out of marketing communications

5. Sharing Your Data

We do not sell or share your personal data with third parties without your consent, except as required by law or to provide services that you have requested. We may share your data with trusted service providers who help us deliver our services.

6. Security of Your Data

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your personal data. However, no data transmission over the internet can be guaranteed to be 100% secure, and we cannot ensure the security of data transmitted to our website.

7. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When we make changes, we will post the updated policy on this page and update the date at the bottom. Please review this Privacy Policy periodically to stay informed about how we are protecting your data.

8. Contact Us

If you have any questions or concerns about this Privacy Policy, or if you wish to exercise your data protection rights, please contact us at: Contact Us
